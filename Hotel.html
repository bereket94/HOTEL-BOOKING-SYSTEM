Our Story

A Place of Warmth
& Welcome

Bereket Hotel sits in the vibrant city of Hosaina, a crossroads of southern Ethiopian culture and commerce. We offer travelers a comfortable refuge — whether you are passing through, visiting family, or here on business.

Our staff speak Hadiya, Amharic, and enough English to make every guest feel at home. We pride ourselves on genuine Ethiopian hospitality: warm, unpretentious, and heartfelt.

Located at the heart of Hosaina, we are steps from the main road, markets, and public transport that connect you to the wider region.

The Experience

Ethiopian
Coffee Ceremony

No visit to Ethiopia is complete without the coffee ceremony — a ritual of roasting, brewing, and sharing that can last over an hour. At Bereket, we serve fresh Ethiopian coffee daily, brewed the traditional way.

Hosaina sits in the heart of the Hadiya zone, where coffee, culture, and community come together. Let us welcome you to this warmth.
